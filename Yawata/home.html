ABOUT US

YAWATA Co., Ltd. was named after the city of Yawata, which is now part of Kitakyushu City. In the northern part of Kyushu Japan Yawata used to be famous for steelworks, and used to be the location of Yawata Ironworks, an important steel mill that helped develop Japan into an industrialized nation. Yawata began in 1973 when the Lertwilai (Thailand), a leader in welding rods and welding equipment, joins Nippon Steel Welding Products and Engineering Co., Ltd. to establish YAWATA Co., Ltd. together, and with Japanese technology combined with the craftsmanship of Thai people, YAWATA is committed to producing only the highest quality products for customers. Over the past 50 years, YAWATA has helped develop the industry in Thailand by producing welding rods for a wide range of industries ranging from construction, transportation, petrochemicals to the energy industry.
